## Use cases

- **Competitor pricing landscape** — price points, promotional cadence, depth in your category
- **Price elasticity research** — measurement methodology + category benchmarks
- **Promotional calendar benchmarks** — how often + how deep by category
- **Subscription pricing research** — tier structures, grandfathering, migration patterns
- **B2B SaaS tier structures** — good-better-best design, pricing metric choice
- **Dynamic pricing research** — algorithmic pricing, surge pricing, personalized pricing

## Opinionated source priorities

**Prioritize:**

- **Hermann Simon pricing research** — Simon-Kucher consulting, books
- **Harvard Business Review pricing** — academic + operator case studies
- **Professor Russell Belk, Adrian Ryans, Madhav Kaul** — academic pricing research
- **Operator voice on LinkedIn** — real pricing moves + outcomes
- **Open Pricing research** — open-source + open-community pricing data
- **Price Intelligently (ProfitWell) research** — SaaS pricing data
- **SEC filings** — public companies disclose pricing + promotional trends

**De-prioritize:**

- Vendor-published "pricing strategy" content
- Generic listicles
- AI-generated pricing advice

## Question-set specializations

### For competitor pricing landscape:

1. Top 10 competitors: price points, tiers, bundles
2. Promotional cadence — frequency + depth observed
3. Pricing communication — where do they communicate price?
4. MAP enforcement evidence
5. Recent pricing moves (past 12 months) + visible impact
6. Subscription / recurring pricing if applicable

### For price elasticity research:

1. Methodology choices — conjoint, A/B testing, natural experiments, market-level
2. Category benchmarks for elasticity
3. Cross-price elasticity (cannibalization between SKUs)
4. Promotional elasticity vs. list-price elasticity
5. Willingness-to-pay research techniques

### For promotional benchmarks:

1. Promotional revenue share at peer brands (where disclosed)
2. Cadence norms by category
3. Depth norms — first-time, subscribe-and-save, BFCM, clearance
4. Black Friday / Cyber Monday / holiday category norms
5. Loyalty-tier exclusive pricing

### For subscription pricing:

1. Tier structures — freemium, usage, seat, outcome
2. Grandfathering vs. migration strategies
3. Price-change communication patterns
4. Churn elasticity to price changes
5. Competitive repricing responses

### For dynamic pricing:

1. Algorithmic pricing adoption by category
2. Surge pricing legality + brand-perception impact
3. Personalized pricing technology + risk
4. Real-time price optimization platforms
