## Use cases

- **Creator tool evaluation** — Grin / Aspire / Creatable / Captiv8 / #paid / Klear / Impact / LTK / ShopMy
- **Category creator landscape** — who are the 50-100 best creators for your category? Tier, niche, audience, engagement, reach, sentiment
- **Competitor creator teardown** — who does your competitor partner with? At what volume, tier, deal structure?
- **Platform strategy research** — where to invest: IG vs. TikTok vs. YouTube vs. newsletters vs. TikTok Shop
- **Contract structure research** — market rates by tier, platform, deliverable, usage-rights
- **Whitelisting / paid-amplification benchmarks** — measured CAC / ROAS vs. branded paid at peer brands

## Opinionated source priorities

**Prioritize:**

- **HypeAuditor, Modash, Creatable, Grin benchmark reports** — data-heavy, published annually
- **Creator-economy analysts** — Signal Fire, Peter Yang, Rex Woodbury — publish market-level analysis
- **Creator-platform earnings + leaks** — Meta, ByteDance (private), Google, YouTube Shorts, LinkedIn creator updates
- **DTC operator podcasts** — 2X eCommerce, Operators, Shopify Masters — creator program segments
- **Agency white papers** — larger agencies publish case studies with named brands + results

**De-prioritize:**

- Influencer-marketing agency "thought leadership" (sales bias)
- Creator-selling-to-creator course content
- AI-generated "top 10 influencer strategies" articles

## Question-set specializations

### For creator tool evaluation:

1. Creator discovery — search depth, audience-data quality, fraud detection
2. Outreach automation — CRM functionality, template personalization
3. Contracting + payments — automated contracts? Pay-flows? Tax (1099) handling?
4. Content approval workflows — review, revision, go-live tracking
5. Performance measurement — UTM / discount code attribution, platform integrations
6. Whitelisting / paid amplification — native support or bolt-on?
7. Pricing — per-seat, per-creator, per-message, enterprise tier
8. Scale ceiling — works at 10 creators/month or 1000?
9. Data export — can you get raw data out?

### For category creator landscape:

1. Top-50 creators in category by audience × engagement × brand alignment
2. Platform mix — where does the category live?
3. Engagement benchmarks — likes/followers, comments/likes, shares
4. Typical partnership rates by tier, platform
5. Competitor density on top creators — who else is partnering with them?
6. Emerging creators — up-and-coming, underpriced
7. Creator-owned brand activity — any creators who launched brands in your category?

### For platform strategy:

1. Audience fit — is your buyer on this platform?
2. Content format fit — does your product story tell well in this format?
3. Creator supply — is there deep creator bench, or thin?
4. Measurement quality — how clean is attribution on this platform?
5. Platform monetization — TikTok Shop revenue potential, IG Shopping, YouTube Shopping?
6. Brand-safety profile — category sensitivity, moderation history
7. Competitor intensity — who's dominant, who's missing?

### For whitelisting research:

1. Measured ROAS delta: whitelisted vs. branded paid at peer brands
2. Platform mechanics — how IG / Meta / TikTok handle it
3. Contract language — what usage rights are standard
4. Attribution pattern — how platforms attribute whitelisted vs. branded
5. Creative longevity — does whitelisted content fatigue faster or slower?
