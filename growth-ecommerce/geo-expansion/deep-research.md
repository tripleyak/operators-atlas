## Use cases

- **Target-market prioritization research** — which markets first, based on what scoring?
- **Market-size + category research** for specific countries
- **Regulatory / compliance research** — product compliance, tax, data-privacy for target market
- **Local competitive landscape** — who's in the market, how are they positioned
- **3PL + fulfillment vendor evaluation** for specific geography
- **Local payment-method research** — which methods matter, coverage vs. usage
- **Culture + localization research** — language, color, humor, visual, claim dynamics

## Opinionated source priorities

**Prioritize:**

- **Country-specific trade bodies / chambers of commerce**
- **Official government statistics** — EU Eurostat, UK ONS, Stats NZ, Stats Canada, Australian Bureau of Statistics, INEGI (Mexico), IBGE (Brazil)
- **Local industry associations** — category-specific
- **Google consumer insights** by country
- **Euromonitor International** — category sizing (paid)
- **Statista** by country (paid tier for real data)
- **Local legal firms' published compliance guides** — often free, authoritative
- **International-expansion case studies** from operators — 2X eCommerce international episodes, Shopify Plus case studies

**De-prioritize:**

- Agency-published "how to enter X market" listicles
- Generic international-expansion consulting frameworks
- AI-generated localization content

## Question-set specializations

### For market prioritization:

1. Market size + growth trend for category
2. Competitive density + concentration
3. Regulatory complexity — product compliance, tax, labeling, data privacy
4. Payment infrastructure — local methods, acceptance of global methods
5. Fulfillment accessibility — 3PL options, customs complexity
6. Brand / category fit — cultural resonance, existing demand signals
7. Operating cost — local hire costs, marketing CPMs, freight
8. Language + localization effort

### For regulatory research:

1. Product compliance requirements (CE, UKCA, FDA-parallel, PMDA, NMPA)
2. Tax regime — VAT / GST / consumption tax rates, registration thresholds
3. Import duties + landed cost calculation
4. Data privacy regime — consent architecture requirements
5. Labeling + claims requirements — language, units, allergens, marks
6. Advertising regulations — claims, endorsements, comparative advertising
7. Consumer protection — return rights, warranty requirements, distance-selling rules

### For local competitive landscape:

1. Top 10-15 competitors in category in target market
2. Pricing architecture — local vs. imported
3. Retail + DTC + marketplace mix
4. Marketing channel mix — paid, organic, creator
5. Local vs. global competitors — who's winning
6. Recent market entries — who launched, how did it go

### For 3PL evaluation:

1. Country coverage + local warehouse presence
2. Customs + duties expertise
3. Integration with home-market systems (Shopify / ERP)
4. Pricing — storage, pick/pack, shipping tiers
5. Returns handling — local return address, processing
6. Category expertise (cold-chain, fragile, hazmat if applicable)
7. Customer-reference brands + track record

### For payment-method research:

1. Top 5-10 payment methods by country by usage
2. Acceptance + integration via current payment processor
3. Cost difference vs. global methods
4. Fraud-rate differences
5. Chargeback / dispute rules
