## Use cases

- **Launch landscape research** — who's launched in your category recently, what did they do, how did it go?
- **Launch playbook research** — DTC launch patterns, retail launch sequences, Amazon launch case studies
- **Competitor-launch teardown** — creative, channels, pricing, PR at a specific competitor launch
- **Category demand validation** — is there enough demand to warrant launch? (pre-commit research)
- **Pricing research for launch** — competitor pricing, price-anchor analysis, promotion depth norms
- **Channel-mix research for launch** — what channel mix succeeded / failed at peer brands?
- **Retail launch research** — Target / Whole Foods / Sephora / REI / specialty channel entry playbooks

## Opinionated source priorities

**Prioritize:**

- **S-1 filings from recently-public DTC / consumer brands** — launch sequences, revenue growth curves, channel mix disclosed
- **Operator podcasts** — 2X eCommerce, Operators Podcast, Shopify Masters, Honest Ecommerce, The Business of Brand Operators
- **DTC trade press** — ModernRetail, Retail Brew, Morning Brew, Glossy
- **Category-specific trade publications** — Beauty Matter (beauty), SGB Media (sports/outdoor), Progressive Grocer (CPG)
- **Launch case studies from retail partners** — Shopify Plus, Klaviyo, Recharge, Tapcart case-study libraries
- **Amazon Product Launches KB** for any Amazon-component launch
- **Retail buyer LinkedIn and press** — buyers at Target / Whole Foods / Sephora publish about category bets

**De-prioritize:**

- Vendor-published "5 launch tips" content
- Generic LinkedIn launch advice
- AI-generated "how to launch" content

## Question-set specializations

### For launch landscape / competitor teardown:

1. Recent launches in category (12-24 months) — brands, products, timing
2. Launch-day channel mix — paid, organic, PR, influencer
3. Pricing strategy — launch price, ongoing price, promotion structure
4. Inventory strategy — pre-order, waitlist, limited-drop, full-catalog?
5. Retail presence — DTC-only, retail-parallel, retail-after, retail-first?
6. PR execution — embargo managed, media-list breadth, specific outlets
7. Influencer / creator strategy — scale, vetting, content structure
8. Day-0 / Day-30 / Day-90 revenue disclosure (if public) — size of ramp
9. Review velocity + rating early
10. What went wrong / early failures — (operator postmortems, founder interviews)

### For category demand validation:

1. Category size + growth trend (last 3 years)
2. Competitor count + density
3. Pricing dispersion — is there price discipline or discount war?
4. Retail attention — buyers actively merchandising the category?
5. Search volume trends — Google Trends, Keyword Planner
6. Social conversation volume — TikTok hashtag volume, Reddit threads, LinkedIn discussion
7. Funding / acquisition activity — VC bets, strategic acquisitions
8. Macro trends — demographic, regulatory, cultural tailwinds/headwinds

### For pricing research:

1. Category price range + distribution (low / median / high)
2. Premium anchor brands' pricing
3. Launch pricing vs. ongoing pricing patterns
4. Promotion depth norms (discount % + frequency)
5. Subscription discount conventions
6. Retail MSRP vs. DTC pricing policy patterns

### For retail launch research:

1. Target retailer's category buyer cadence + criteria
2. Typical lead time from pitch to PO
3. Standard wholesale margin + chargeback structure
4. MAP policy norms in category
5. Co-op marketing budgets + requirements
6. Sell-through expectations first 90 days
7. Recent launches at that retailer — what worked
