## Use cases

- **ICP benchmarking research** — what's the ICP at peer B2B SaaS / services companies?
- **ABM platform evaluation** — Demandbase / 6sense / Terminus / Rollworks
- **Intent data platform evaluation** — Bombora / 6sense / ZoomInfo Intent / Slintel
- **Sales engagement platform** — Outreach / Salesloft / Apollo / Groove / Reply
- **B2B content engine benchmarking** — content velocity, format mix, MQL conversion at peer brands
- **Cadence / sequence benchmarks** — email open rates, reply rates, meeting-set rates
- **Pipeline attribution methodology** — multi-touch vs. time-decay vs. W-shaped

## Opinionated source priorities

**Prioritize:**

- **SaaStr** — operator voice, SaaS benchmarks, pipeline-building talks
- **Lenny's Newsletter** — PLG + B2B growth
- **Forrester B2B research** — demand gen benchmarks
- **Gartner B2B research** — ABM + intent platform evaluations
- **Bridge Group SDR benchmark reports** — SDR productivity, tools, pay structures
- **TOPO / Tenbound** — SDR-specific research
- **Heinz Marketing, Ceros, TrustRadius** — B2B-specific operator content
- **Earnings calls of public B2B SaaS** — HubSpot, Salesforce, ZoomInfo; disclose benchmarks

**De-prioritize:**

- Vendor-marketed "why we're better" whitepapers (sales bias)
- AI-generated LinkedIn "top 10 B2B tips"
- Agency-published case studies without verifiable numbers

## Question-set specializations

### For ICP benchmarking:

1. How peer companies define ICP — firmographic + behavioral + fit scoring
2. ICP scoring models in use
3. ICP precision vs. volume tradeoffs
4. Common ICP failures (too loose / too tight)
5. ICP calibration cadence (quarterly? annually?)

### For ABM platform evaluation:

Use `deep-research-technical-reference.md` specializations:

1. Account identification + intent
2. Advertising capability (programmatic, paid social, display)
3. Sales enablement (account intelligence to reps)
4. Measurement (account-level pipeline attribution)
5. Integration depth (CRM, MAP, sales engagement)
6. Pricing — tier, by-user, by-account
7. Customer results at named brands
8. Implementation time + complexity

### For cadence / sequence benchmarks:

1. Email open rates by industry
2. Reply rates by step, by channel
3. Meeting-set rates by segment
4. Optimal cadence length (days + touches)
5. Personalization depth impact
6. LinkedIn vs. email vs. phone mix
7. Best-performing subject line / opening patterns

### For pipeline attribution:

1. Multi-touch model choices (linear, time-decay, W-shaped, U-shaped, custom)
2. First-touch vs. last-touch tradeoffs for long sales cycles
3. Offline / event attribution integration
4. Opportunity-influence vs. source credit
5. Marketing-to-sales credit allocation methodology
