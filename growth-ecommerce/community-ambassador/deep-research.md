## Use cases

- **Platform evaluation** — Discord / Circle / Slack / Facebook / Mighty Networks / native
- **Ambassador-platform evaluation** — Grin / Aspire / Crewfire / Brand Ambassador
- **Competitor community teardown** — Notion community, Figma community, Peloton community, etc.
- **Community health benchmarks** — posting rates, engagement, moderator ratios by size + category
- **Ambassador economics research** — compensation benchmarks by tier, ROI measurement
- **Crisis moderation case studies** — how brands have handled community controversies

## Opinionated source priorities

**Prioritize:**

- **CMX Hub** — community-manager research + benchmarks
- **Orbit / Common Room** — community-software-published research
- **Operator voice on LinkedIn + Twitter** — named community managers sharing data
- **Platform-published case studies** — Discord, Circle, Slack case studies of branded communities
- **Harvard Business Review on communities** — long-format community strategy
- **Academic research on online community dynamics**

**De-prioritize:**

- Community-platform sales decks
- Generic "engage your audience" listicles
- AI-generated community advice

## Question-set specializations

### For platform evaluation:

1. Target audience comfort with platform
2. Moderation tools + admin capabilities
3. Integration with commerce / CRM / marketing stack
4. Content discoverability (search, threading, tagging)
5. Member profile + recognition systems
6. Monetization (if gated community)
7. Data access — can you export member data?
8. Pricing model
9. Community scale ceiling

### For competitor community teardown:

1. Platform choice + rationale
2. Channel / thread structure
3. Moderator presence + style
4. Content cadence (brand vs. member)
5. Event cadence
6. Ambassador program structure (if visible)
7. Member engagement tier distribution
8. Brand tone in community

### For ambassador economics:

1. Compensation benchmarks by tier
2. Typical output (content, referrals, events) per tier
3. ROI measurement at peer brands
4. Tier-mobility pathways
5. Contract structures (exclusivity, commitment, IP)
6. Measurement challenges + solutions

### For health benchmarks:

1. Posting rate (% of members posting per month) by community size
2. Response rate to questions
3. New-member activation rate
4. Moderator:member ratio
5. Churn / inactivity patterns
